# Ownership Extraction Guide

## Goal
Mention people only when it helps the user identify who to ask next for missing context.

## Include people when:
- they repeatedly explain a component or relationship
- they appear to make or defend a decision
- others defer to them on a system area
- they seem to own or operate the relevant component
- they are the clearest follow-up contact for unresolved ambiguity

## Do not include people when:
- they are merely present in the meeting
- they speak without adding ownership or domain signals
- naming them adds clutter without action value

## How to describe ownership
Prefer cautious language such as:
- “appears closest to…”
- “seems to own…”
- “may be the best person to ask about…”
- “appears to be the main explainer for…”

## Avoid
- asserting firm ownership without support
- turning the report into a people summary
- listing names disconnected from components or missing context
